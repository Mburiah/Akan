GHANA AKAN NAMING SYSTEM

Akan names are derived from Ghananian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Many people celebrate their birthdays but it is a hard task to calculate the exact day of the week they were born. In this website, I have made it possible to get the right information with an additional fun Akan name. Have fun trying out our beautiful Akan names web app!
